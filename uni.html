The Soul of the Universe

Thunder with drops of due,

Trinkets of hope blossoming;

A Rorschach of rainbowed hue!

 

Beseeching, I asked the drenched Cloud;

'Who are you?'

And then, the grand Universe spoke!

'I am,

The wonder called Life,

The splendour named Tide.

The beauty of the Ocean,

Unfurling far and wide!

 

ÔªøCooking a concoction of Love,

The Sun, the Moon, and the golden nectar above.

The tripping Sand, the mystical Mountains,

The Hills with rains,

And bleeding charmed chains.

 

Weeping with Joy, the Truth, the Folly,

The Fables, the Noise.

Reaping the seeds of glittering Sunbeam,

I am, the essence, the silver net,

The feathered vision of your bronzed dream.

I am Existence!

Blistering like frivolous flakes of snow.

I am Liberation!

Fanning the magma on the nape of your bow.

 

I am You,

And all, that ignites life within You!

 

- Yavanika Kaushal
