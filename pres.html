Spirit of the nation!

Madam President, Smt. Draupadi Murmu Ji, 15th President of the Republic Of India.

 

Achievements or loss have boiled down to her surrendering to the greatness of the spirit of the collective. 

 

For the people! Always the people! 

***

 

Her life's story is instrumental.

 

It feels right that she has been bestowed the opportunity to serve her purpose, to which she dedicates her life. 

 

Balance!

***

 

We all are scratched, tethered, torn and broken. 

That's a strength though, every scar stitches to our story, every lesson to our glory. We as a race have come a long way, have evolved and prospered. 

***

 

Cheers to our home, the liberty of evolution, for the freedom of collectivity,

To the spirit of the nation!

 

Reflections,

-Yavanika 💕💕
