Ode to the Love called Home

When I see you at the brim of a lightyear!

I cling to the vision,

So that you never forget me,

Because I'll always have you in my heart.

 

The calm the clatter and my masquerade,

you'll watch me through with glee and praise.

You know me, behind the darkness that lurks and lays;

 

You are my glimmer of life! My every strife!

My victory you are, and you are my might.

 

Look how far I've come?

Aren't you proud of me?

You're proud I know!

 

You are the home to where I float,

my universe in the fist,

my guiding hope!

 

You live within me,

In my fear and my freedom,

You are mine.

-By Yavanika Kaushal
