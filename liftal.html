They talk of a life new born,

But I see a life re-written.

 

The night was as blue as day,

And the day as dark as nightfall,

The sun shone with pale lament,

While the crimson moon,

Soared to glory,

Something, unchanged, were the woods,

Luminous,

Crescent hopes shadowing desires.

 

The ship sailed into the firmament,

While the space calm,

At the quiet dispatch.

 

Fantasy it was,

Laden with bruised prophecies,

And cruel wanton, devouring

Painful ecstasies.

 

And then they say,

It was a life newborn,

But I see a world re-written.

- Yavanika Kaushal
