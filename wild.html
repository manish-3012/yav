Transient Wilderness

The Life that was once magic

Transcends into charisma

Then a mystery

Then into the master's timeless beauty

The wilderness

 

The freedom of Nature

Dwelling as the Cloud

Pouring in the rain 

And merging with the earth

Dew, dust, and pain

 

All colour a picture, 

Sprinkled with truth

Murmuring Joy with delight

 

Look at that wildflower

The smile it breaks

Makes me wonder in amazement

What strength and courage it takes

Which is, but a gentle soul

With delicate petals like wings unfold

Yet shadows the universe 

With mirth and wisdom 

far beyond its mold

 

The turquoise net with twinkling dew 

Covering Life for the blooming river

Isn't that a great sight?

That's when the forest comes alive

 

My inward eye, both flashing and bright

With shades of pure Splendor

Just watching in bliss

The Life of earth 

Spreading its wings

Like the Phoenix of heavenly grandeur

 

-Yavanika Kaushal
