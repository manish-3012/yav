In the Rain

Million drops of hope splashed,

Glowing on my forehead,

Flowing streams of unbound glory,

That's life elevated!

I saw myself dancing,

Flying,

Wild freedom possessing,

Every sprinkle of life becoming me,

And only me.

 

- Yavanika Kaushal
